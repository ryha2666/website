A bit about me

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the edge of analysis and hardware — where the model gives you a prediction and the test either confirms it or tells you exactly what you got wrong. That feedback loop is what keeps me in aerospace.

My work spans both ends: I've written performance prediction tools in MATLAB and C++, run trade studies on aircraft geometry, fabricated prototype wings, and executed rocket engine test protocols. I want to keep building that range — contributing to real hardware programs where analytical work connects directly to what gets built and tested.
