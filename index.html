A bit about me

I am an Aerospace Engineering senior at CU Boulder with a strong interest in aircraft structures, aerodynamics, UAV design, and propulsion systems. My engineering work has focused on applying classroom fundamentals to real aircraft and rocket projects through CAD modeling, MATLAB analysis, hands-on testing, and iterative design.

I am drawn to open-ended problems where the analytical work connects directly to what gets built and tested. Whether it is running trade studies on a wing geometry or operating valve controls during a hot fire, I want to be involved across the full cycle from concept to hardware.
